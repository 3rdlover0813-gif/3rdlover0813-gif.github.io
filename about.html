I'm a freshman in Technological Systems Management (TSM) at SUNY Korea, Stony Brook University's Korea campus. I'm drawn to the places where engineering, mathematics, and human systems overlap — which is also why a visual-rhetoric course feels relevant: interfaces and images are systems that persuade.

I'm especially interested in a mathematics specialization and the idea of building tools that make complex systems easier to read. An earlier project of mine looked at AI-based traffic-signal optimization for urban congestion, which connects to the UN Sustainable Development Goals on industry & infrastructure (SDG 9) and climate action (SDG 13).
